The History of Tampa Bay

Discover how trade, innovation, and community shaped Tampa Bay into the vibrant area it is today. Come and explore the rich history of Tampa Bay at the Tampa Bay History Center. Where you can dig through ancient archives and dive into history to discover the past. Tampa Bay History Center has much to offer. Come and Join us.
