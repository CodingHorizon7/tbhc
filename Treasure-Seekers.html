Treasure Seekers Exhibit

Treasure Seekers Exhibit

The Treasure Seekers Exhibit immerses visitors in the adventurous history of pirates, explorers, and treasure hunters who sailed the Gulf Coast and Tampa Bay. Explore interactive displays featuring recovered artifacts, historic documents, and stories of daring expeditions. Discover the region’s maritime history and uncover secrets of hidden treasures that shaped Florida’s coastline and culture.

Guests can explore interactive maps, try on period costumes, and view replicas of historic ships. The exhibit offers hands-on activities for all ages, making history come alive in a fun and engaging way. Learn about the lives of those who risked everything in search of fortune and adventure in the early days of Florida’s maritime history.
