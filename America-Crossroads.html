The Guitar & a Changing Nation

America at the Crossroads: The Guitar & a Changing Nation

Explore how a single instrument reshaped culture, identity, and social movements across America. “America at the Crossroads” traces the journey of the guitar — from folk and blues roots to modern genres — showing how it became a powerful symbol of creativity, resistance, and unity.

The exhibit highlights iconic guitars, historic photographs, and stories of legendary musicians. It delves into social changes, musical revolutions, and how the guitar served as a voice for generations, reflecting cultural shifts and connecting communities across regions.

Visitors will experience how musical innovation, social history, and cultural evolution intersect. The guitar becomes not just an instrument, but a lens to understand America’s changing identity — making this exhibit a tribute to music’s power to transform societies.
