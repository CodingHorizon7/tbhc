Columbia Café Experience

Enjoy classic Cuban dishes and Florida culinary history at the museum café.

About the Columbia Café

The Columbia Café inside the Tampa Bay History Center brings the flavors and traditions of the original Columbia Restaurant to downtown Tampa. The café serves a curated selection of Spanish- and Cuban-inspired dishes that made the Ybor City restaurant famous.

Menu & Prices

  • Spanish Bean Soup - $6.99
  • 1905 Salad - $9.99
  • Cuban Sandwich - $12.99
  • Media Noche Sandwich - $13.99
  • Empanadas (Beef or Chicken) - $7.99
  • Café con Leche - $4.50
  • Flan - $5.50

Hours of Operation

Open daily: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM (slightly extended on weekends). Offers dine-in, reservations, and curbside pickup.

Located on the Tampa Riverwalk, the Columbia Café offers a charming waterfront dining atmosphere, perfect for visitors looking to enjoy local cuisine and relax before or after exploring the museum.
