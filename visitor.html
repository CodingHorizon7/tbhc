Planning a Visit

The Tampa Bay History Center is open daily from 10 AM to 5 PM. Located on the Tampa Riverwalk, the museum features immersive exhibitions that explore 12,000 years of Florida history.

Enjoy group tours, family activities, research archives, and a fresh dining experience at the Columbia Café.

Whether you're a first-time visitor or a returning guest, we welcome you to discover the stories that shaped Tampa Bay.
