Touchton Exhibit

The Touchton Exhibit highlights the fascinating history of Tampa Bay’s early settlers and pioneers. Visitors can explore interactive displays that showcase historic artifacts, documents, and stories that bring the region’s rich past to life. Learn about the families, trades, and cultural traditions that shaped the growth of Tampa Bay.

From household items and tools to photographs and maps, the exhibit offers a detailed glimpse into daily life during the early development of the area. Discover the innovation, resilience, and spirit of the people who helped build the foundation of the community we know today.
